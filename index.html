Why always being richer has to be synonim of success?

We want to develop a new game design, that will make you think again the rule of the game. In the game, as in the life, if you do, buy, produce more it is always seen as a positive thing. We don't fully agree with this idea, so we propose a shift in the paradigm.

Should we consider richer who has more money?

Another aspect that we want to take into consideration, is that trees are important for the environment. What they "produce", oxygen, is the most valuable thing we have, without which we cannot survive! Isn't this better than just money? Shouldn't we measure richness in trees?
